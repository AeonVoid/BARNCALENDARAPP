User Guide

This app will allow you to see the different upcoming events your favorite studios are hosting!
To add a studio:
Go to All Studios, and click on the icon cooresponding to the studio. Then, click to check the box on the "Add to My Studios" button.
To remove a studio:
Either go to My Studios or All Studios and click on the icon cooresponding to the studio. Then, click to uncheck the box on the "Add to My Studios" button.
To check your studios:
Go to My Studios to check your favorited studios. You can check their upcoming events by clicking on their cooresponding icons.
